Explanation Of Our Company Profile

About CV. Toho Sukses Mandiri

CV. TOHO SUKSES MANDIRI is the sole/official distributor for SUGIMURA brand oil/lubricant products operating in Indonesia under PT. Sugimura Chemical Indonesia with more than 60 years of experience as the main supplier of industrial and automotive oil needs in Japan and throughout the world. We sell various kinds of oils/lubricants using Base Oil Group 2 at affordable prices.

High-quality products, comprehensive after-sales service and excellent technical support are the features that make Sugimura Oil your ideal partner for industrial lubricant and coolant solutions.

About PT. Sugimura Chemical Indonesia

PT Sugimura Chemical Indonesia, which was founded in March 2013, is a 50-50 Joint Venture between Sugimura Chemical Industrial Co. LTD and PT. Adiguna Eka Sentra (PT. Indochemical Citra Kimia group company).

Located in the Industrial Area in Cikarang, PT. Sugimura Chemical Indonesia has a warehouse capacity of more than 800MT for materials and 800MT for finished products, as well as an in-house blending capacity of up to 600MT per month.
